I work at the intersection of AI research, practical engineering, and business outcomes — helping leadership teams navigate AI adoption with clarity and confidence.

My focus is on **Retail, Loyalty, and Industrial domains**, where I lead the advisory for AI implementation and consulting engagements. Each engagement ends with evidence-based recommendations your leadership team can actually act on.

Based in **Hyderabad**, I've been building and writing about software since 2009 — from early experiments with .NET and Python to large-scale martech platforms, and now to the AI transformation reshaping every layer of the stack.

## What I do

I help organisations answer the hard questions around AI: what to build, what to buy, where the real risks are, and how to measure outcomes that matter. That means getting into the technical details when necessary and stepping back to the business case when that's what the room needs.

Current focus areas:

- AI strategy and implementation advisory for retail and loyalty businesses
- Agentic AI systems — design, evaluation, and responsible deployment
- Translating AI research into practical engineering decisions
- Helping technical and non-technical leadership get on the same page

## Writing

I've been writing here since 2009 — across technology, entrepreneurship, and the long arc of building software products. More recently, the writing has shifted toward AI: weekly roundups, deep dives on new models, and reflections on where all of this is heading.

I'm Hitesh — founder of [OptCulture](https://optculture.com), where we build tools that help retailers understand and engage their customers better.

I've spent most of my career thinking about a deceptively simple question: *what makes a customer want to come back?* Not because of a discount or a push notification, but because the experience itself was worth returning to.

That question sits at the intersection of technology, behavioral psychology, and product design — which is where I tend to spend most of my time.

Before starting OptCulture, I worked in retail analytics and saw firsthand how the distance between data and action destroys good intentions. Companies collect everything. They act on almost nothing. The gap isn't technical — it's organizational and design. OptCulture is my attempt to close that gap.

---

I'm based in Bengaluru. I'm interested in data infrastructure, behavioral economics, B2B product design, and how businesses earn — and lose — trust over time.

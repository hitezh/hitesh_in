Most AI projects fail for boring reasons: the wrong use case, data that was not ready, or a vendor bet made before anyone validated it. An advisory hour is the cheapest way to avoid that.

Bring a problem or an idea. We will talk through the use case, your data readiness, and the real risks, and I will point you to the simplest pilot or MVP that can prove value, or tell you honestly if AI is not the answer yet.

## What I can help with

- **AI Advisory** — strategy, feasibility, ROI modeling, vendor assessment, and roadmaps grounded in real engineering constraints, often as a fractional Head of AI.
- **AI Executive Training** — a six-week program that turns your leadership team AI-native, with hands-on workflows, governance, and a 90-day adoption roadmap.
- **MVP Development** — the smallest prototype that answers the key technical and business questions, across agents, computer vision, and ML.
